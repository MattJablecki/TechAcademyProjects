The Sweatshop:A Retrospective






The Sweatshop was an underground music venue/skate spot existing from Late 2008-Early 2010 in the Olneyville neighborhood of Providence, RI.




Listed below are all of the shows hosted at the Sweatshop, including links to the artists, flyers, and photographs where available. All performers are from Providence, RI unless otherwise noted.




Located at the bottom of the page, you will find instances of the Sweatshop appearing in film and television.
